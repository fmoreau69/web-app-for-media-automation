About - WAMA Synthesizer

Automatic generation of speech audio from text.

What is it?

  • An interface to enter or upload text and generate speech audio
  • Backed by text_to_speech_synthesizer with fallback engine support

How does it work?

  • Enter text or upload a document and start synthesis
  • Choose language and voice options
  • Follow processing and download the generated audio file
