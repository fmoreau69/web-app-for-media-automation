WAMA : Webapp for Automatic Media Anonymization

WAMA detects and blurs faces and number plates quickly and accurately.

How To Use It?


What can I upload?

  • Photos
  • Videos
  • Medias folder

Setting options:

  • blur faces (True/False)
  • blur plates (True/False)
  • blur people (True/False)
  • blur cars (True/False)
  • conf (conf value)
  • boxes (True/False)
  • show labels (True/False)
  • show conf (True/False)
  • show (True/False)
