## Analysis Framework

### 1. Current State Assessment
- What is the current architecture?
- What are the main components and their responsibilities?
- How do components communicate?

### 2. Problem Identification
- What are the architectural bottlenecks?
- What patterns are being misused?
- Where is technical debt accumulating?

### 3. Recommendations
- What changes would improve the architecture?
- What is the priority of each change?
- What are the trade-offs?
